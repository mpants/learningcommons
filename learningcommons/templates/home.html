What is a Learning Commons?

Dozens of workshops are taught in your community each year. Book clubs and discussion groups; crafters and gardeners; the retired professor who you always see at the grocery store. Online, a simple search can turn up free online courses, video tutorials and DIY blog posts, open journal articles. Learning is in abundance.

A commitment to lifelong learning shouldn't have to mean a trap door to debt. In the US, student loan debt is now greater than credit card debt, even as jobs disappear and transform rapidly enough to render too many degrees obselete.
