Learning Commons

Outside of Universities and Schools, what are the sources of learning in a community? What kind of learning can we participate in if we can't afford college, because of money or time? How can our community serve as a source of learning and accreditation, leading to better self-reliance, confidence, employment, and collaboration?

Version 1 of this site is simply an attempt to aggregate local sources of learning in a community and allow members of the community to post their own. Meant to serve as a local portal for learning. Ideally, if it's completed, it will be deployable in other communities.

This version originates in Carrboro-Durham, NC, and is inspired by conversations with Jacob Levin at Weaver Street Market.

Thank you to all sentient beings.
